Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2025

App Name: Merit Ledger

Developer: Merit Ledger Development Team

Contact: meritledger@163.com

1. General Provisions

We take your privacy and personal information protection very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use Merit Ledger (the "App"), as well as your legal rights.

Please read and understand this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App. By starting to use or continuing to use the App, you agree to all terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address (for registration, login, and identity verification)
  • Password hash (for email login only; we do not store your plain text password)

We only use your email address for authentication and data security.

2.2 Third-Party Login Information (if you choose to use Google account login)

  • Login provider (Google Sign-In)
  • Google account ID (for account association)
  • Nickname and avatar (provided by Google)
  • Email address (provided by Google)

2.3 Usage Data

  • Record content you create in the App (text content of merit/demerit records)
  • Images you upload (up to 3 per record)
  • Audio you record (up to 60 seconds)
  • Basic behavioral data such as usage time and frequency

2.4 Device and Log Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version number
  • Basic log information (for troubleshooting and service improvement)
Important: We do not actively collect personal information unrelated to the App's functionality. We do not collect sensitive information such as your location, contacts, or SMS messages.

3. Purpose of Use

We use your personal information only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining core App features (recording merits/demerits, data statistics, etc.)
  • User authentication and account management
  • Data synchronization and backup (if you enable data sync)
  • Improving product experience and features
  • Compliance with legal requirements

3.1 Data Synchronization

  • If you enable data sync, your record data (including text, images, and audio) will be uploaded to our servers
  • Data synchronization uses encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
  • You can disable data sync at any time in Settings
  • After disabling data sync, data on the server will be deleted, but local data will remain

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Methods

  • Local Storage: Some data is stored on your device (if cloud sync is disabled)
  • Cloud Storage: If data sync is enabled, related data will be stored on servers

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS) to protect data transmission security
  • Passwords are stored using hash encryption; we cannot access your plain text password
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability scans
  • Restricted employee access; only authorized personnel can access user data

4.3 Data Retention Period

  • Account Information: During your use of the App and 30 days after account deletion
  • Record Data: During your use of the App; deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Verification Codes: Deleted immediately after use; automatically deleted after 24 hours if unused
  • Log Information: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5.1 We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • As required by laws, regulations, or regulatory authorities
  • To protect the legitimate rights and interests of the App or users

5.2 Third-Party Service Providers

To provide login services, we share necessary information with the following third-party service provider:

  • Google (Google Sign-In) - for Google account login

Google has its own privacy policy. We recommend that you review their privacy policy. We only share the minimum information necessary for login authentication.

6. Your Rights

You have the following legal rights:

  • Right to Access: Access your personal information
  • Right to Rectification: Correct or delete your personal information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Account Deletion: Delete your account
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent (without affecting completed processing)
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain a copy of your data (where applicable)

6.1 Account Deletion Process

  1. In the App: Settings → Account Management → Delete Account
  2. Via email: Send a deletion request to privacy@Merit Ledger.com
  3. We will complete account deletion and data deletion within 7 business days of receiving your request
  4. After account deletion, all data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered

7. Minors

This App is not intended for users under the age of 16 (for users outside China and Japan).

If you are a minor, please use this App with the consent and guidance of your guardian. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a minor, we will immediately delete the relevant data.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

The App may use the following technologies:

  • LocalStorage: Stores your login status and app preferences
  • SessionStorage: Temporarily stores session information
  • Device Local Database: Stores your record data (if cloud sync is disabled)

You can clear this information by clearing app data or uninstalling the App.

9. Data Security Incidents

If a personal information breach or loss occurs, we will:

  1. Immediately activate emergency response procedures
  2. Notify affected users promptly (if possible)
  3. Report to relevant regulatory authorities (where applicable)
  4. Take necessary measures to minimize losses

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. After updates, we will notify you through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Website announcements
  • Email notifications (if you have provided an email address)

For significant changes, we will obtain your consent again.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Your consent

Your GDPR Rights

Data Transfers
